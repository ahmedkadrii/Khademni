About Us

Welcome to Khademni, the premier job recruitment platform in Tunisia.

Our Mission

To connect job seekers and employers, fostering a productive and engaging job market in Tunisia.

Our Vision

To become the leading platform for job recruitment, enhancing career opportunities and economic growth.

Our Values

Integrity, innovation, and inclusivity are at the heart of everything we do.

Why Choose Khademni?

For Job Seekers

  • Access to a wide range of job listings across various industries.
  • Easy-to-use search and application process.
  • Personalized job recommendations based on your profile.
  • Resources and tools to help you succeed in your job search.

For Employers

  • Post job openings quickly and easily.
  • Find qualified candidates through advanced search and filters.
  • Streamline your recruitment process with our tools.
  • Enhance your employer brand with a dedicated company page.

Our History

Khademni was founded in 2024 with a vision to revolutionize the job recruitment process in Tunisia. Since our inception, we have rapidly grown to become the go-to platform for both job seekers and employers, offering innovative solutions and unparalleled service. Our journey has already been marked by significant milestones, including the launch of our mobile app in 2025, reaching 1 million users in 2026, and expanding our services to include career counseling and resume building in 2027.

Our Achievements

Milestones

  • 2010: Khademni was founded.
  • 2015: Launched our mobile app, making job search easier on the go.
  • 2018: Reached 1 million users milestone.
  • 2020: Expanded services to include career counseling and resume building.

Awards

  • 2016: Best Job Recruitment Platform at the Tunisia Tech Awards.
  • 2019: Innovation in Employment Services by the Tunisian Business Council.
  • 2021: Excellence in Customer Service by the Tunisian Ministry of Labor.

Community Involvement

At Khademni, we believe in giving back to the community. We are actively involved in various social initiatives aimed at improving the job market and supporting economic development in Tunisia. From organizing job fairs and career workshops to collaborating with educational institutions, we strive to make a positive impact on society.

Future Plans

Looking ahead, Khademni is committed to continuous innovation and growth. We plan to introduce new features such as AI-driven job matching, expanded employer branding tools, and enhanced career development resources. Our goal is to make the job search and recruitment process even more efficient and effective for all our users.
